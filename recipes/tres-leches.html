Tres Leches Cake

Description

This recipe will teach you how to cook a tres leches cake, a moist cake made using milk, evaporated milk and condensed milk, creating a really sweet tasting dessert.

  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder, (6 g)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

  • 1 cup granulated sugar, , divided (200 g)
  • 1/3 cup whole milk, (80 ml)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, (5 ml)
  • 12 ounce can evaporated milk, (354 ml)
  • 14 ounce can sweetened condensed milk, (396 ml)
